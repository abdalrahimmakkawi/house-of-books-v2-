Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

House of Books ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our digital book summary platform.

We are committed to transparency and will never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and login
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through our payment provider (we do not store credit card details)

Reading Data

  • Reading Progress: Books you've read, currently reading, or want to read
  • Reading Notes: Personal notes you create for books (premium feature)
  • AI Chat History: Your conversations with our AI book assistants
  • Reading Streaks: Your daily reading habits and achievements

Usage Analytics

  • Platform Usage: Features you use and how you interact with our service
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for service optimization
  • Performance Data: Loading times, error reports to improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain your House of Books account
  • Deliver book summaries and AI chat functionality
  • Track your reading progress and personalize your experience
  • Enable community features and discussions

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Optimize performance and fix technical issues
  • Conduct A/B testing for feature improvements

Communication

  • Send important account notifications
  • Provide customer support responses
  • Share relevant updates about new features (optional)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing and secure login processes
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Access Controls: Limited access to user data by authorized personnel only

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your reading data, personal notes, and account information remain private.

Limited Sharing

We only share data in these specific circumstances:

  • Payment Processing: With our payment provider to process subscriptions
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services essential for operation (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Community Features

Information you choose to share in community discussions may be visible to other users. You control what you share publicly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Reading Data: Preserved to maintain your reading history and progress
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal information is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Data: Access your reading history and account information
  • Edit Information: Update your account details and preferences
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and associated data
  • Export Data: Download your reading notes and progress (premium feature)

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Control notification preferences in your account settings
  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link in each message

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage to improve our service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some features.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Children's Privacy

House of Books is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
